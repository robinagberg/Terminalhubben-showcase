#  Terminalhubben

## AI Operations Platform for Warehousing & Logistics

Terminalhubben is an AI-powered operations platform designed for modern warehouses, distribution centers, and logistics organizations.

The platform combines AI agents, productivity intelligence, voice-first workflows, real-time communication, and operational analytics into a unified ecosystem for warehouse operators, supervisors, and management teams.

---

## Vision

Traditional Warehouse Management Systems (WMS) focus on transactions.

Terminalhubben focuses on people, decisions, and operational intelligence.

Our mission is to transform daily warehouse activity into actionable insights that improve productivity, efficiency, and decision-making in real time.

---

## Platform Overview

### Galaxy Workspace

The operational layer for warehouse personnel.

Features include:

* Galaxy AI Warehouse Copilot
* Voice-first workflows
* Operational updates and alerts
* Team communication
* Scanner AI
* Outbound logistics tools
* Performance tracking

Designed to support operators directly on the warehouse floor.

---

### Productivity Intelligence

The platform's operational analytics engine.

Analyzes:

* Picked order lines
* Productivity metrics
* Operator performance
* Voice feedback
* Operational comments
* Bottlenecks
* Capacity utilization

Transforms raw operational data into meaningful recommendations and business insights.

---

### Galaxy Enterprise

The management and intelligence layer.

Includes specialized AI agents for:

* Operations Manager AI
* WMS Expert AI
* Productivity Analyst AI
* Forecasting & Analytics

Provides leadership teams with a centralized command center for operational visibility and decision support.
